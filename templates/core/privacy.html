Privacy

This is an open-source self-hostable application. Behaviour depends on the operator of the instance you are using.

Data we process

  • The text you submit is sent to the configured TTS engine (edge-tts or gTTS) to produce audio.
  • For quota and rate limiting, we record the IP address and a per-day character counter in volatile cache.
  • A short preview of the submitted text (first 500 characters), the voice ID, format, and IP are stored in the database for analytics/admin purposes.

Audio file retention

Generated audio files are automatically deleted after AUDIO_FILE_TTL_SECONDS (default 2 hours).

No accounts, no tracking

This application does not create user accounts, does not set analytics cookies, and does not share data with third parties beyond the upstream TTS provider invoked for synthesis.

Your rights

You own the generated audio. Operators of an instance should provide a contact method for data deletion requests.
